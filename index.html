2026 is tracking as a breakout year
Q1 2026 shows 41 deals and $8.19B in capital — 46% more volume and 64% more capital than Q1 2025. If this pace holds, 2026 will challenge 2021 as the firm's peak year. This insight does not exist inside the firm today without a real-time dashboard.
